Subject: Introducing RecruitPro: Our New Recruitment Management System

Dear Recruitment Team,

We are excited to introduce **RecruitPro**, our new centralized platform designed to streamline our hiring process and enhance the candidate experience.

**What RecruitPro Can Do For You:**

*   **Centralized Job Management:**
    *   **Post & Edit Jobs:** Easily create and manage job listings with details like requirements, location, and closing dates.
    *   **Control Visibility:** Keep jobs as "Drafts" while working on them, "Publish" them to the public board when ready, and "Archive" them when finished.
    *   **Duplicate Listings:** Quickly copy existing job posts to create similar openings.

*   **Efficient Application Tracking:**
    *   **Dashboard Overview:** See key metrics like total applications, pending reviews, and shortlisted candidates at a glance.
    *   **Status Management:** Move candidates through stages (Pending -> Reviewed -> Shortlisted -> Rejected) with a simple dropdown.
    *   **Automated Emails:** The system automatically notifies candidates when you receive their application and when you update their status (e.g., Shortlisted).

*   **Candidate Review:**
    *   **Review CVs:** Instantly view candidate resumes (PDFs) directly in the browser without downloading files.
    *   **Filter & Search:** Quickly find candidates or jobs using the built-in search and status filters.

*   **Analytics:**
    *   Access real-time charts showing application trends, top-performing job posts, and candidate status breakdowns.

We believe RecruitPro will significantly reduce administrative overhead and help us identify top talent faster.
